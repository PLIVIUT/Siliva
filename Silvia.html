Built For Fun... and perfection.

The S15 Silvia debuted in 1999 from Nissan as the last Silvia. The S15 has a smaller body than its predecessor, the S14, which enabled it to run more like the S13. The headlights with hanging eyes give the car a sporty look and along with the compact yet powerful body shape were popular features among car enthusiasts, particularly with the younger crowd.
